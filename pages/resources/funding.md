# Funding and Project Support Opportunities

  We list here a number of funding opportunities related to COVID-19 reseearch and activities to fight the pandemic and its effects. Opportunities related to computing resources (e.g. HPC or HTC allocations) are listed separately on [Computing Resources](https://science-responds.org/resources/computing) page.

  * US National Science Foundation (NSF)
    * [Dear Colleague Letter on the Coronavirus Disease 2019 (COVID-19)](https://www.nsf.gov/pubs/2020/nsf20052/nsf20052.jsp)

  * Creative Destruction Lab (CDL)  
    * The Creative Destruction Lab is a non-profit organization that delivers
      programs for seed-stage science- and technology-based companies. The have setup
      a [recovery program](https://www.creativedestructionlab.com/program/recovery/)
      specifically for products and services that public health and economic
      challenges posed by COVID-19. The program will be global and online, with an
      accelerated schedule as compared to the usual program.

  * National Sciences and Engineering Resource Council of Canada (NSERC)
    * [NSERC Alliance COVID-19 Grants](https://www.nserc-crsng.gc.ca/Innovate-Innover/COVID-19/index_eng.asp) are being made available to projects related to COVID-19 research in Canada.
